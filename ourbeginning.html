ABOUT US

Here it begins; this is to introduce ourselves as the Raman Academy. Now if there are any questions regarding what that is and where it belongs to, the answer is that it’s a sports academy which belongs to one of the most wholesome places in Bengaluru, which is C. V. Raman Nagar. At the outset, it has just about given the team all the exposure necessary to be proficient in sports and more. Coming from lower middle class back ground, there was very little support from the families as it is a common parent’s worry that sports may not yield a successful career. Once done with graduation, like all other individuals, it was expected from parents to go to work, have a stable pay, get settled and pass on the same lifestyle to our next generation. But at Raman Academy, the team aims to think beyond such worldly notions. The intent is to give young kids an opportunity to pursue their career in sports and break the stereotypes. Hence it was envisioned that the Raman Academy be set up in order to train the kids back home at C. V. Raman Nagar. Having significantly established themselves in the football fraternity, it was planned to have teams across all age categories with a focus to make every member adept at the game. C. V. Raman Nagar hosts all the infrastructure required for a sportsperson, be it any sport. In fact, the most astounding quality of every member in the Raman club is the deftness acquired in multiple sports from a young age which makes it so much easier to understand a young kid's mindset and channel the path required. The resolve is on setting up strong basics in the game so that kids will find it easy when they go out to any other clubs or sports complex. Progressing from a basic level to multiple levels, the Raman Academy centrally aspires to take the kids' future to greater heights.

A huge thanks goes out to Ganesh for his shear hard work and perseverance in building the team. It would be incomplete without mentioning the support shown by decathlon in conceptualizing the Academy. To begin with, Ganesh was working at Decathlon where he set up a voluntary football coaching camp for the kids to free them off their academic stress. He was multitasking by coaching kids in football and working in Decathlon. A resident from Brigade approached Ganesh after seeing his good work and gave the opportunity to coach at Brigade and after which there has been no turning back. It started with a handful of kids but has now grown into a huge family including the grateful supportive parents in there. Initially it was tough to get adjusted to the environment but with time, the kids were transformed to the next level.

The academy has now successfully completed 2 years in Football coaching at Brigade and has received multiple opportunities to coach in and around Bangalore. It has been a gratifying saga since there is a pioneered club with all age categories in addition to coaching in Brigade due to which time just flies. Now, the time has come for the academy to go full time into coaching and there is enough support from every member of the team and it's all about the passion we moreover don’t think that we are working but doing what we really dreamt off. So we our very passionate about producing some good sports person to our country and compete at any level. Now we have an opportunity to act as Physical education Faculty at preschools, Raman club is really excited about the challenge, since we have already have what it takes to be it, Since we have worked at Brigade with kids starting with 4years, Now it gets even tougher since at preschools we have Kids from 2-6years but as told earlier it’s our passion so it gets that easier. Looking forward for the challenge.
