FOOTBALL AT RAMAN SPORTS ACADEMY
The Sports Academy undertakes coaching overseen by highly experienced and qualified coaches for children of all age groups and skill levels.
OUR BELIEFS AND THE BENEFITS
Through sport we know that children learn the importance of values such as discipline, respect and team spirit. We help them build such good character with the right attitude.
FITNESS OF ALL INDIVIDUALS
We even have programs for working professionals to improve their fitness through the sport of football. The programs are tailored to guide you towards overall fitness.
VENUES AND OTHER EVENTS
We are involved with different residential complexes and schools as well for maximum reach. We even conduct regular tournaments and events for the benefit of everyone.

OUR STORY

Keeping the dream alive

The Raman Sports Academy is the brainchild of two youthful and brilliant footballers, Ganesha and Vidyasagar. They left behind their corporate life and started their journey to provide children a professional coaching system from the grassroots level to help them cater to their interest in the sport and take it up professionally.

Football the way it is intended to be

We have designed our programs in a way that young boys and girls learn, play and develop progressively through fun exercises, small-sided games and exciting competition. The goals of the program are to help them attain the skills of effective communication, goal setting and problem solving to help them as individuals.

AND A LITTLE MORE ABOUT US

We actively conduct sessions for football and fitness for children and adults. Our sessions occur at different facilities in the city of Bangalore. A glimpse of what you may encounter in one of our sessions can be seen below.

THE SPORTS ACADEMY

We have a 5 level structured program for football handled by various individuals who mentor children of all skill levels and age through a module structure. They allow the children to build their character on the basis of the Academy's beliefs. High emphasis is placed on overall player development so that child can fulfill their potential and grow further.

OUR OFFERINGS

Our modules promote development from grassroots to advanced level. The different age groups for coaching are 13+, U-13, U-10, U-8 and U-6.

BASICS FIRST APPROACH

Every session is split into different sections from warmup to attacking play to defensive play. This provides the children with a strong base.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

After the child has a strong foundation in the basic skills, they are encouraged to practise role related skills to help them give their maximum on the pitch.

INDIVIDUAL ATTENTION

We strictly maintain a recommended coach to children ratio and beyond that pay keen attention to detail of every child in order to help them to the maximum extent.

FITNESS PROGRAMS

The outdoor fitness program for adults is an easy-to-follow, scientifically based, exhilarating way to exercise. Whether you're looking to start something new or to mix up your current routine you will feel your body and your soul elevate. The program doesn't require any kind of equipment and is completely outdoors.

NON-LINEAR APPROACH

The program includes movement in all directions for maximum effect and increases stability in muscles and joints while burning more calories.

GOING GREEN

Using any form of indoor equipment curbs us to the capability of the equipment. The green outdoors are the most superior gym imaginable.

TIME EFFICIENT

People short on time look for quick options to obtain results. Our workouts are an option for improving fitness in limited time through outdoor activities.

INDIVIDUAL ATTENTION

It is never late to include any form of fitness into one's lifestyle. We helpl individuals adopt it and adapt their life around it for best results.
